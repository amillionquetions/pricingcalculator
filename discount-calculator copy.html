What is a discount calculator?

A discount calculator helps you work out the final price of a product after a discount is applied. It also shows how much money is saved.

How to calculate discount

The basic formulas are:

Savings = Original Price × Discount Percentage

Final Price = Original Price - Savings

This makes it easy to see the true sale price and compare offers quickly.

Example of a discount calculation

If an item costs £100 and has a 25% discount, your savings are £25 and the final price is £75.

Why discount analysis matters for shoppers and businesses

Discount analysis helps shoppers understand whether a deal is worthwhile and helps businesses plan promotions more effectively. It is useful for sales campaigns, pricing decisions, and comparing offers.
