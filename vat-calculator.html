VAT Calculator

Calculate VAT instantly. Add VAT to a price or remove VAT from a total.

What is VAT?

VAT (Value Added Tax) is a consumption tax applied to goods and services. Businesses charge VAT on sales and can reclaim VAT on purchases.

How to calculate VAT

To add VAT: multiply the amount by (1 + VAT rate). To remove VAT: divide the total by (1 + VAT rate).
